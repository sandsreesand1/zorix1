Uniforms

Uniform Solutions by Zorix – Elevate Your Brand Identity

At Zorix, we are dedicated to crafting uniforms that not only meet industry standards but also embody the unique identity of your organization. Our uniform collections serve diverse sectors, ensuring each piece aligns with your brand image and functional requirements

Corporate Uniforms:

Our corporate uniforms enhance your brand identity through tailored suits, jackets, pants, skirts, shirts, ties, scarves, and more. Every design prioritizes professionalism and comfort, making a lasting impression while providing a TailorFit experience.

School Uniforms:

We design customized school uniforms for kindergartens, nurseries, schools, colleges, and universities. Schools can select from a variety of options, including:

  • Shirts, pants, shorts, t-shirts
  • Sports uniforms
  • Teacher coats and costumes
  • Fancy dresses and graduation gowns

Hospitality Sector Uniforms:

Tailored for hotels and restaurants, our hospitality uniforms reflect the distinct identity of each establishment. We offer uniforms for:

  • Front Desk
  • Food & Beverage staff
  • Housekeeping
  • Chefs
  • Maintenance and Security teams
  • Salon and Spa professionals

Medical Sector Uniforms:

Our medical uniforms are designed with functionality and hygiene in mind, suited for hospitals, clinics, pharmacies, and labs. Our range includes:

  • Lab coats and doctor coats
  • Nurse uniforms and scrubs

Construction & Industrial Sector Uniforms:

We support the construction and industrial sectors with durable, safety-oriented attire, including:

  • Coveralls and overalls
  • Safety vests, helmets, and shoes
